# 福州麻将 (Fuzhou Mahjong) - Comprehensive Rules Research

> Sources: Baidu Baike, Wikipedia (Chinese), Tongcheng You (同城游), QQ Games, 91Game/17173, Bianfeng Games (边锋游戏), Sina Blog, and various Chinese gaming forums. See full source list at bottom.

---

## 1. Basic Setup (基本设置)

### Tiles (牌的组成)
- **Total**: 144 tiles
- **Suited tiles (序数牌)**: Wan (万), Bing/Tong (饼/筒), Tiao/Suo (条/索) - each 1-9, 4 copies = 36 each, total 108
- **Honor tiles (字牌)**: Wind tiles (东南西北) x4 each = 16; Dragon tiles (中发白) x4 each = 12; total 28
- **Flower tiles (花牌)**: Spring/Summer/Autumn/Winter (春夏秋冬), Plum/Orchid/Bamboo/Chrysanthemum (梅兰竹菊) = 8

### Key Fuzhou Mahjong Feature
**All honor tiles AND flower tiles are treated as "flowers" (花) for scoring purposes.** In Fuzhou Mahjong, 东南西北中发白 are NOT playable tiles - they are immediately set aside as flowers and replaced, just like 春夏秋冬梅兰竹菊. This means **only the 108 suited tiles (万饼条) are used for actual gameplay**, and there are a total of **36 "flower" tiles** (28 honor + 8 traditional flowers).

Some online platforms offer two modes: **20-flower mode** (only using a subset) and **36-flower mode** (using all honor + flower tiles). The traditional/standard version uses all 36.

### Players
- 4 players
- Each player builds a wall of **18 stacks** (36 tiles)

## 2. Game Flow (游戏流程)

### 2.1 Determining the Dealer (定庄)
- First game: randomly selected (or determined by dice roll)
- Subsequent games: if the dealer wins (胡牌), they continue as dealer (连庄). If a non-dealer wins, the dealership passes counterclockwise. If the round is a draw (荒牌/流局), the dealer continues.

### 2.2 Dice Roll and Dealing (掷骰子与发牌)
1. The dealer rolls two dice
2. Count counterclockwise from the dealer to determine which player's wall to start drawing from
3. From that player's wall, count from right to left by the dice sum to determine the starting position
4. Players take turns drawing 2 stacks (4 tiles) at a time, for 4 rounds
5. The dealer draws one extra tile (跳牌)
6. **Result**: Dealer has 17 tiles, other players have 16 tiles each

### 2.3 Flower Replacement (补花)
1. Starting from the dealer, going counterclockwise, each player sets aside all honor tiles and flower tiles face-up in front of them
2. Players draw replacement tiles from the **tail end** of the wall (牌尾), one tile per flower tile set aside
3. **If replacement tiles drawn are also flowers**: the player must wait until all four players have completed their current round of flower replacement, then a new round begins (starting from the dealer, skipping players who didn't draw new flowers)
4. This process repeats until no player has any flower/honor tiles in hand - only 万/饼/条 remain

### 2.4 Revealing the Gold (开金)
1. After all flower replacement is complete, flip the first tile from the tail end of the wall face-up - this is the **Gold tile (金牌)**
2. The Gold tile is moved to and displayed face-up on the **9th stack from the end** (倒数第9墩)
3. **If the revealed Gold tile is a flower/honor tile**: it counts as the dealer's flower, the dealer draws a replacement, and a new tile is flipped as the Gold
4. The Gold tile determines which tile type serves as the wild card for this round. **All 4 copies** of that tile type (minus the one displayed) become Gold tiles (3 remaining Gold tiles in play)

### 2.5 Play Phase (出牌阶段)
1. The dealer plays first by discarding a tile
2. Play proceeds counterclockwise
3. Priority order for claiming discards: **Hu (胡) > Peng/Gang (碰/杠) > Chi (吃)**
4. Only the player to the discarder's right (下家) can Chi; any player can Peng or Gang
5. Rule: "在同一圈内，不能弃先胡后" - within the same turn cycle, you cannot pass on a Hu and then Hu later in the same cycle

## 3. Flower Tile System (花牌系统) - DETAILED

### 3.1 What Counts as a Flower
ALL of these are set aside as flowers and scored:
- **Wind tiles (风牌)**: 东(East) x4, 南(South) x4, 西(West) x4, 北(North) x4
- **Dragon tiles (箭牌)**: 中(Red) x4, 发(Green) x4, 白(White) x4
- **Season tiles**: 春(Spring), 夏(Summer), 秋(Autumn), 冬(Winter) - 1 each
- **Plant tiles**: 梅(Plum), 兰(Orchid), 竹(Bamboo), 菊(Chrysanthemum) - 1 each

### 3.2 Flower Scoring (花分计算)
- **Each individual flower tile = 1 flower point (花)**
- **Collecting 4 identical flowers = 6 flower points** (not 4). This applies to:
  - 4x East (四个东) = 6 flowers
  - 4x South (四个南) = 6 flowers
  - 4x West, 4x North, 4x Red, 4x Green, 4x White = 6 flowers each
  - Spring+Summer+Autumn+Winter (春夏秋冬一套) = 6 flowers
  - Plum+Orchid+Bamboo+Chrysanthemum (梅兰竹菊一套) = 6 flowers
- The "4 identical flowers" bonus is sometimes called **花杠 (flower gang)** because it functions similarly to a hidden gang in terms of tile retention

### 3.3 Flower-Position Relationship (正花/偏花)
Some sources mention a traditional correspondence between seat position and flowers:
- **East seat (东)**: 梅(Plum) + 春(Spring)
- **South seat (南)**: 兰(Orchid) + 夏(Summer)
- **West seat (西)**: 竹(Bamboo) + 秋(Autumn)
- **North seat (北)**: 菊(Chrysanthemum) + 冬(Winter)

If the flower matches your seat, it's a **正花 (zheng hua / matching flower)**; otherwise it's a **偏花 (pian hua / off flower)**. However, in most modern Fuzhou Mahjong implementations, this distinction does NOT affect scoring - all flowers count equally as 1 point each.

### 3.4 Gangs and Flower Equivalence
- **Ming Gang (明杠/exposed kong)** = 1 flower point
- **An Gang (暗杠/concealed kong)** = 2 flower points
- Only the **winning player's** gangs count for scoring
- The "4 identical flowers = 6 points" rule also counts as an An Gang for the purpose of **tile retention** (留牌)

## 4. Gold Tile System (金牌系统) - DETAILED

### 4.1 Basic Gold Rules
- The Gold (金) acts as a **wild card / joker (百搭/财神)** - it can substitute for ANY suited tile
- There are **3 Gold tiles** in play (the 4th copy is displayed face-up as the indicator)
- Gold tiles are used to complete sets (顺子/刻子/对子) in your hand

### 4.2 Restrictions on Gold Tiles
- **Gold tiles CANNOT be Chi'd (吃), Peng'd (碰), or Gang'd (杠)** by other players
- **Gold tiles CANNOT be used to Hu** when discarded by another player (听牌者不能胡金牌)
- **If you discard a Gold tile**: you are restricted to **self-draw wins only (只能自摸)** for the rest of the round - you cannot win on others' discards (不能平胡/放胡)
- A discarded Gold tile does NOT count as a flower point

### 4.3 Gold in Scoring
- Each Gold tile in the winning hand counts as **1 flower point** (same as a regular flower)
- Gold points are added to your flower total

### 4.4 Grab Gold (抢金)
This is a special instant-win condition that occurs at the **very beginning** of the round:

**Dealer Grab Gold (庄家抢金)**:
- After the Gold is revealed, the dealer checks if swapping the Gold into their hand would complete a winning hand
- If yes, the dealer discards one useless tile and takes the Gold, declaring a win
- This only works with the dealer's **first discard**

**Non-dealer Grab Gold (闲家抢金)**:
- After the dealer makes their first discard, the next player draws. If the drawn tile is a Gold tile and it completes their hand, they can declare Grab Gold
- This only works with the non-dealer's **first drawn tile**

**Priority**: 天胡 (Heavenly Win) > 闲家抢金 (Non-dealer Grab Gold) > 庄家抢金 (Dealer Grab Gold)

If multiple non-dealers can Grab Gold, the one closest counterclockwise to the dealer has priority.

**Scoring**: Grab Gold = **30x base score**

### 4.5 Special Gold Win Types

#### Three Gold Down (三金倒 / 三头金)
- If any player collects **all 3 Gold tiles** in their hand, they can declare an **immediate win** regardless of hand composition
- No need to have a valid hand pattern
- Can only be achieved through self-draw (自摸)
- **You cannot use "about to complete Three Gold Down" as a basis for Grab Gold** (上手有两张金牌，不能以和"三金倒"的和型来抢金)
- **Scoring**: 40x base score

#### Gold Sparrow (金雀)
- Using **2 Gold tiles as the pair (将/雀)** in a winning hand, with the remaining tiles forming valid sets
- When multiple players are waiting for the same tile, **Gold Sparrow takes priority** regardless of seating position
- **Scoring**: 60x base score

#### Gold Dragon (金龙)
- Having **3 Gold tiles forming a triplet (刻子)** in a winning hand - the Gold tiles are used as themselves (their actual tile identity), NOT as wild cards
- The rest of the hand must also form valid sets
- This is the rarest and highest-scoring special win
- **Scoring**: 120x base score

#### Key Distinction: Three Gold Down vs Gold Dragon
- **Three Gold Down**: 3 Golds = instant win, no hand pattern needed, 40x
- **Gold Dragon**: 3 Golds used as a triplet of their own tile type, full valid hand required, 120x

## 5. Winning Rules (胡牌规则) - COMPLETE

### 5.1 Basic Hand Patterns (基本牌型)
A winning hand consists of 17 tiles (including the winning tile) arranged into:
- 1 **Pair (将/雀/对子)**: 2 identical tiles (11)
- 5 **Sets (面子)**: each being either a Sequence (顺子: 123) or Triplet (刻子: 111) or Kong (杠子: 1111)

The 5 valid combinations are:
1. 11 + 123 + 123 + 123 + 123 + 123 (pair + 5 sequences)
2. 11 + 123 + 123 + 123 + 111 + 111 (pair + 3 sequences + 2 triplets)
3. 11 + 123 + 123 + 111 + 111 + 111 (pair + 2 sequences + 3 triplets)
4. 11 + 123 + 111 + 111 + 111 + 111 (pair + 1 sequence + 4 triplets)
5. 11 + 111 + 111 + 111 + 111 + 111 (pair + 5 triplets = 对对胡)

**Note**: Kongs (1111) count as triplets for pattern purposes.

### 5.2 Ways to Win
- **Self-draw (自摸)**: Drawing the winning tile yourself
- **Discard win (放胡/点炮)**: Winning on another player's discard

### 5.3 Complete List of Special Win Types

| Win Type | Chinese | Description | Multiplier |
|----------|---------|-------------|------------|
| Heavenly Win | 天胡/天和 | Dealer wins immediately after Gold is revealed, before discarding | 30x |
| Grab Gold | 抢金 | Winning by incorporating the Gold tile on the first action | 30x |
| Flat Win (no flowers, no gangs) | 平胡 (无花无杠) | Winning with zero flower tiles and zero gangs | 30x |
| Flat Win (one flower) | 平胡 (一张花) | Winning with exactly one flower tile and zero gangs | 15x |
| Three Gold Down | 三金倒 | Collecting all 3 Gold tiles (instant win) | 40x |
| Gold Sparrow | 金雀 | 2 Gold tiles used as the pair | 60x |
| Gold Dragon | 金龙 | 3 Gold tiles used as a triplet (not as wilds) | 120x |

**Important**: If multiple special win types apply simultaneously, **only the highest multiplier is used** (不累加，取最大值).

### 5.4 Additional Win Types (Extended/House Rules)

These are found in some versions and may be considered optional/regional variants:

- **对对胡 (All Triplets)**: All 5 sets are triplets/kongs + pair. In this pattern, Gold tiles **cannot** be used as wild cards UNLESS two Golds form the pair (金雀).
- **清一色 (Pure One Suit)**: All tiles are from a single suit. Gold tiles **can** still be used as wild cards even if the Gold tile's actual suit differs from the hand's suit.
- **单吊 (Single Wait)**: All tiles have been Chi'd/Peng'd/Gang'd except one - waiting to draw the matching tile for the pair.
- **七对 (Seven Pairs)** and **十三幺 (Thirteen Orphans)**: These are **NOT part of standard Fuzhou Mahjong rules**. They belong to National Standard (国标) or other regional variants.

### 5.5 Winning Priority (胡牌优先级)
When multiple events compete:
1. 天胡 (Heavenly Win) - highest priority
2. 闲家抢金 (Non-dealer Grab Gold)
3. 庄家抢金 (Dealer Grab Gold)
4. Regular Hu by seating order (counterclockwise from discarder)
5. 金雀 (Gold Sparrow) - overrides seating order for the same discard

### 5.6 One-Shot Multiple Winners (一炮多响)
Fuzhou Mahjong generally does **NOT** allow multiple winners on one discard. Only one player wins, determined by counterclockwise proximity to the discarder. **Exception**: Gold Sparrow (金雀) takes priority over seating position.

## 6. Scoring System (计分系统) - DETAILED

### 6.1 Score Components

The score is built from these components:
- **Base score (底分)**: Fixed at 1 (but increases with 连庄)
- **Flower score (花分)**: Sum of all flower points (flower tiles + gang equivalents + gold tiles)
- **Gold score (金分)**: Each Gold tile in hand = 1 point (counted within flower total)
- **Lian Zhuang (连庄/consecutive dealer) bonus**: Adds to the base

### 6.2 Flower Score Calculation
```
Flower Points =
  (number of individual flower/honor tiles)
  + (number of 4-of-a-kind flowers × 6, replacing the 4 individual points)
  + (number of Ming Gang × 1)  [only winning player's gangs]
  + (number of An Gang × 2)    [only winning player's gangs]
  + (number of Gold tiles in hand × 1)
```

### 6.3 Lian Zhuang (连庄) Calculation
- First time as dealer (站顶): base = 1 (no bonus)
- First successful defense (连庄1次): base = 1 + 1 = 2
- Second successful defense (连庄2次): base = 1 + 2 = 3
- Nth successful defense: base = 1 + N

The 连庄 bonus applies when the dealer wins. When a non-dealer wins, the non-dealer receives the 连庄 bonus from the dealer as extra winnings.

### 6.4 Scoring Formulas

**Version A (QQ Games / some platforms):**
```
Discard Win (放胡/点炮):
  Score = (花分 + 金 + 连庄数 + 5) × 2 + max(特殊胡法倍数)

Self-draw (自摸):
  Score = [(花分 + 金 + 连庄数 + 5) × 2 + max(特殊胡法倍数)] × 3
```

**Version B (Alternative/simplified):**
```
Discard Win:
  Score = 花分 + 金 + 连庄数 + 杠 + max(特殊胡法倍数)

Self-draw:
  Score = (花分 + 金 + 连庄数 + 杠) × 2 + max(特殊胡法倍数)
```

**Version C (Multiplier-based, some platforms):**
```
Base = 底分(3) + 金数 + 字数 + 明杠数 + 暗杠数×2 + 连庄×2 + 字杠×2
Then multiply by win type: 平和×1, 自摸×2, 三金倒×3, 金雀×4, 抢金×5
```

**Note**: Different platforms and local house rules use slightly different formulas. The core concept is consistent: base + flowers + gold + lian zhuang + special bonus, with self-draw multiplying the result.

### 6.5 Payment Rules (付钱规则)

**Self-draw (自摸)**:
- The other **3 players each pay** the winning player the calculated score
- Total winnings = Score × 3

**Discard win (放胡/点炮)**:
Two variants exist:
1. **Traditional (一家放冲三家赔)**: The discarder pays for all three losers (pays 2x the score; or some versions: discarder pays 3x)
2. **Modern/Online (谁放冲谁负责)**: Only the discarder pays, but pays **2x the calculated score**

### 6.6 Bao Pai / Liability (包牌规则)
Some house rules include:
- **三进要赔 (Three-feed liability)**: If one player feeds (gives Chi opportunities) to the same opponent three times, and that opponent wins, the feeder must pay for all three losers
- **清一色包牌**: When a player has Chi'd/Peng'd three times in the same suit (making it obvious they're going for Pure One Suit), if someone then discards into their win, the discarder pays for all three

## 7. Chi, Peng, Gang Rules (吃碰杠规则)

### 7.1 Chi (吃)
- Can only Chi from the player to your left (上家)
- Form a sequence (顺子) with 2 tiles from your hand + 1 discarded tile
- Lowest priority (below Peng, Gang, and Hu)

### 7.2 Peng (碰)
- Can Peng any player's discard
- Requires 2 identical tiles in hand + 1 discarded tile
- Higher priority than Chi

### 7.3 Gang (杠)
Four types:
1. **Ming Gang from discard (明杠/直杠)**: 3 tiles in hand + 1 discarded tile
2. **Ming Gang from Peng (补杠/加杠)**: Already Peng'd 3 tiles + draw the 4th yourself
3. **An Gang (暗杠)**: All 4 identical tiles drawn by yourself
4. **Hua Gang (花杠)**: Collecting all 4 of the same honor tile (e.g., 4x East)

After any Gang, draw a replacement tile from the **tail end** of the wall.

### 7.4 Gang Scoring (only for the winner)
- Ming Gang = 1 flower point
- An Gang = 2 flower points

### 7.5 Robbing a Kong (抢杠胡)
- When a player attempts a **补杠 (supplement kong)** - adding a 4th tile to an existing Peng - other players CAN declare Hu on that tile
- This counts as a **discard win (点炮)** by the player who attempted the kong
- Note: This applies to 补杠 only, NOT to An Gang or direct Ming Gang

## 8. Draw / End-of-Round Rules (流局/荒牌规则)

### 8.1 Tile Retention (留牌/留尾)
- Base retention: **18 tiles** remain undrawn (including the displayed Gold tile)
- For each **Ming Gang**: retain **1 additional tile** (+1)
- For each **An Gang**: retain **2 additional tiles** (+2)
- For each **花杠 (4 identical flowers)**: treated as An Gang, retain **2 additional tiles** (+2)
- The Gold tile display position shifts forward accordingly when retention count changes

### 8.2 Final Draws (最后四张)
- The last 4 drawable tiles are drawn in turn by the players
- During these final draws, **no more flower replacement or discarding occurs**
- If no one self-draws during these final tiles, the round is a **draw (荒牌/流局)**

### 8.3 Draw Consequences
- No winner, no score changes
- The **dealer continues** as dealer for the next round (庄家继续坐庄)

## 9. Special Situations (特殊情况处理)

### 9.1 Drawing Flowers During Replacement
- If you draw a flower tile while replacing flowers, you must **wait for all other players to complete their current replacement round**, then start a new round
- The new round follows the same order (starting from dealer, skipping those who don't need replacement)
- This repeats until no one draws flowers during replacement

### 9.2 Gold Tile is a Flower
- If the tile flipped for Gold is a flower/honor tile, it counts as the **dealer's flower**
- The dealer draws a replacement from the tail
- A **new tile is flipped** from the tail to be the Gold
- This repeats until a suited tile (万/饼/条) is revealed as Gold

### 9.3 Discarding Gold
- The Gold tile CAN be discarded (it's legal)
- However, the player who discards a Gold tile is **punished**: they can only win by self-draw for the rest of the round
- Other players **cannot** Chi, Peng, Gang, or Hu on a discarded Gold tile
- The discarded Gold does NOT count as a flower point

### 9.4 Robbing Kong (抢杠胡)
- **Yes**, Fuzhou Mahjong does support robbing a supplement kong (补杠)
- It counts as the kong-attempting player having discarded (点炮)

### 9.5 Hai Di Lao Yue (海底捞月)
- Drawing the very last available tile and winning counts as a self-draw (自摸)
- **No additional bonus** is given for this in standard Fuzhou Mahjong rules (unlike some other variants)

### 9.6 Multiple Players Wanting to Win (截胡/一炮多响)
- Standard Fuzhou Mahjong: **only one player can win** per discard
- Priority: counterclockwise from the discarder (closest wins)
- **Exception**: Gold Sparrow (金雀) overrides seating priority

## 10. Dealer Rotation (轮庄规则)

- **Dealer wins (庄家胡牌)**: Dealer continues (连庄), 连庄 count +1
- **Non-dealer wins (闲家胡牌)**: Dealership passes counterclockwise to the next player, 连庄 resets to 0
- **Draw (荒牌/流局)**: Dealer continues, 连庄 count is preserved (some variants: +1)
- **One-shot multiple potential winners**: If the situation involves 一炮多响, the player sitting counterclockwise closest to the discarder becomes the winner, and dealership follows accordingly

---

## 11. Summary of Key Fuzhou Mahjong Distinctions

1. **All honor tiles are flowers** - this is the single biggest difference from most other mahjong variants. Only 万/饼/条 are playable tiles.
2. **Gold (金) system** - 3 wild card tiles per round, with a rich set of special wins (三金倒, 金雀, 金龙)
3. **Grab Gold (抢金)** - unique first-action instant win mechanic
4. **Flat Win (平胡)** - having NO flowers is actually rewarded with a 30x bonus (counterintuitive since you'd think more flowers = better)
5. **Discarding Gold penalty** - severely punishes discarding wild cards
6. **No seven pairs, no thirteen orphans** - these standard mahjong hands do NOT exist in Fuzhou rules
7. **Flower Gangs** - collecting 4 of the same honor tile gives 6 flower points instead of 4
8. **18-tile retention** - more tiles are reserved at the end compared to most variants, and this number increases with gangs
